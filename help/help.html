How I can pay?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, etc.), major debit cards, and PayPal for your payment. Unfortunately, we do not accept manual bank transfers. If you use the credit card of a third party (e.g. family member or close friend), please make sure that this person approves your payment.
